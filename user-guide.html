1 · For Customers

1.1 Create an account & sign in

  1. Click Sign In → Create Account in the top-right of the site.
  2. Enter your name, email and a password (minimum 8 characters).
  3. Open the verification email and click the link — then sign in.
💡 Got a referral code from a friend? Open their referral link before registering — you both earn reward points.

1.2 Find products

  • Search bar (top of every page): type at least 2 letters to see live suggestions, brands and products.
  • All categories menu and the quick links: Today's Deals, Lightning Deals, Best Sellers, Fresh, Renewed, Digital, Gift Cards, Live.
  • On a product page you can see photos, price, stock, reviews, questions & answers, and similar products.

1.3 Buy something (step by step)

  1. On the product page choose quantity and press Add to Cart (or Buy Now to go straight to checkout).
  2. Open the Cart (orange button, top-right). Check items, apply a coupon code or gift card if you have one.
  3. Press Checkout. Enter or pick a saved delivery address.
  4. Choose payment: Cash on Delivery (COD) — you pay in cash when the package arrives. The screen shows the exact amount to pay, including the COD handling fee.
  5. Review the order and confirm. You get an order number and a confirmation email.
⚠️ Keep the 5-digit delivery code from your order page — the delivery agent will ask for it at hand-over. Never share it before receiving the package.

1.4 Track your order — with live GPS

  1. Go to My Orders and click the order → Track.
  2. You will see the status timeline: Confirmed → Packed → Shipped → Out for Delivery → Delivered, each with the real time it happened.
  3. The map first shows your delivery destination. The moment the courier starts the route, it switches to their live GPS position — updated every few seconds, with speed.

1.5 Returns & refunds

  • Returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery (unused, original packaging).
  • Go to My Orders → the order → Request Return, choose the reason, and submit.
  • Refunds are processed in 5–7 business days after approval.

1.6 Gift cards, rewards & referrals

  • Buy a gift card at /gift-cards — it is emailed to the recipient instantly with a code.
  • Redeem a code on the same page — the value becomes store credit and applies automatically at your next COD checkout.
  • Reward points (shown in the navbar) come from paid orders, reviews you write, and friends you refer at /referral.

1.7 Get help

  • Click the purple chat bubble (bottom-right) — the assistant answers about orders, returns, payment and more.
  • Type "agent" in the chat to be connected to a human — a support ticket is created automatically.
  • Or open /support for help articles and your tickets.

2 · For Sellers

2.1 Become a seller

  1. Register at /register?role=seller (or ask the admin to change your role to Seller).
  2. Sign in — you land on the Seller Hub (/seller) with your sales analytics.
  3. Set up your shop brand (name, logo, banner) at Seller Hub → Brand.

2.2 Add products

  • One product: Seller Hub → Products → Add New. Fill title, category, price, stock, photos (upload from your device), description.
  • Many products at once: Products → Bulk Upload — download the CSV template, fill it, upload it back. You will see how many were created and any errors.
⚠️ New products need admin approval before customers can see them.

2.3 Handle orders

  1. You get a notification for every new order. Open Seller Hub → Orders.
  2. Press Accept (do this within 24 hours — late orders are auto-reassigned to another seller).
  3. Pack the item, then mark Packed and Ship (within 3 days).
  4. Delivery is finished by a delivery agent with the customer's secret code + photo proof.

2.4 Money — earnings & payouts

  • When a COD order is delivered and cash collected, your share (after the platform commission) is credited to your seller wallet.
  • See balance and history at Seller Hub → Earnings.
  • Add your bank / Khalti / eSewa account there — the admin pays out to that account.

2.5 Grow your shop

  • Brand Registry — apply to register your trademark brand for a verified badge.
  • Ads — create ad campaigns for your products at Seller Hub → Ads.
  • Reviews — read and respond to customer reviews; good service = better placement.

3 · For Delivery Agents

3.1 Getting access

  1. The admin sets your account role to Delivery Agent (Admin → User Management).
  2. When you sign in, you go straight to the Delivery Dashboard. It is also in your account menu (🛵).
  3. You only see orders the admin has assigned to you.

3.2 Delivering an order (step by step)

  1. Open the order card and press 🛵 Start Route — share live GPS. The customer is notified and can watch you approach on a live map. A green LIVE badge shows while sharing.
  2. At the door, press Verify & Collect Cash:
  • Step 1 — Confirm order: check the Order ID matches the package label.
  • Step 2 — Secret code: ask the customer for their 5-digit delivery code and enter it. (5 wrong tries locks the order — admin must unlock.)
  • Step 3 — Photo proof: take a photo of the delivered package. GPS location is attached automatically.
  1. Collect the exact cash amount shown. The app records it and stops GPS sharing automatically.
  2. If the customer refuses or is absent, press ✕ and give the reason — the order is marked failed for the admin to handle.
💡 Allow location permission in your phone browser, or live tracking cannot work.

3.3 Cash rules

  • The amount on screen already includes the COD fee — collect exactly that, nothing more.
  • Your collected total is shown at the top. Hand cash to the office; the admin records the remittance against your orders.

4.2 Daily tasks

  1. Approve new products — Products page → Approve / Reject.
  2. Assign COD orders to agents — COD Management → 🚴 Assign Agent (only delivery-role users appear).
  3. Update order status — All Orders → change status; the customer sees it instantly on tracking.
  4. Answer support — Support page: tickets, escalated chats, help articles.
  5. Reconcile cash — COD Management → Reconciliation tab: select collected orders → Confirm Remittance.
  6. Run payouts — Payouts page → pays sellers from their wallet balance to their saved account.

4.3 Marketing tools

  • Promo Calendar / Coupons — schedule discount codes with dates, usage limits and overlap warnings. Codes work at checkout immediately.
  • Banners — upload homepage hero banners with schedule dates.
  • Push Notifications — send an announcement to all customers or one role.
  • A/B Testing — create an experiment, record visitor/conversion numbers, and the page computes statistical confidence and the winner.

4.4 Safety

  • Fraud Detection flags risky orders (new account + big order + many failed payments…). Approve or block from the page.
  • SLA watchdog runs automatically every day: late sellers are auto-reassigned or cancelled, and you get an alert.
  • Locked delivery codes (5 wrong tries) are unlocked from COD Collections.

5 · Quick Answers (FAQ)

How do I pay?Cash on Delivery — pay when the package arrives. The exact amount is shown at checkout.
Where is my delivery code?On your order page (My Orders → the order). Give it to the agent only at hand-over.
Can I cancel?Yes, before it ships: My Orders → Cancel Order.
How long is delivery?Kathmandu Valley 1–2 days · major cities 2–4 · elsewhere 4–7 business days.
Free shipping?Yes — on orders above Rs. 999.
How do I talk to a human?Type "agent" in the chat bubble, or open a ticket at /support.
How do sellers get paid?Wallet credit after delivery, then payout to their saved bank/Khalti/eSewa account.
Who can open /admin or /delivery?Only accounts with the admin / delivery role. Everyone else is redirected away.
